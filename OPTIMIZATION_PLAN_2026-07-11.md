# Sprunki.tv SEO & content plan

## Baseline

GSC: 56 clicks, 3,874 impressions, 1.45% CTR, average position 61.06. The core `sprunki` term ranks 83.31. The 335-page mixed-game catalog dilutes the site’s stated music-creation topic.

## 0–30 days

- Make the homepage an explicit independent fan/guide resource and add `WebSite`/`VideoGame`/FAQ markup.
- Audit every game URL for unique editorial value, safe/legal embeds and topical relevance. Add `noindex` only after a URL-level decision; do not bulk-index more pages before the audit.
- Make the primary Sprunki hub the internal-link destination for relevant games and guides.

## 31–60 days

- Choose a clear architecture: a focused Sprunki resource (recommended) or a separately branded generic-games property.
- Publish original how-to, character/phase, mod-safety and music-creation content with screenshots and FAQ.

## 61–90 days and KPI

- Monitor indexed-page count and query relevance weekly.
- Get ten focused pages into the top 30 before growing the catalog; target the core term below position 30.
