Roblox: Raft Tycoon is an exciting building and management game where you start with a simple raft and work your way up to creating a magnificent floating empire. Gather resources from the ocean, craft essential items, build structures, and expand your territory. Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start building my raft empire?
▼
You start with a basic raft and essential tools. Gather resources from the ocean, craft items, and gradually expand your raft with new structures and facilities. The more you build, the more options become available!
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! The game features multiplayer functionality where you can collaborate with friends, trade resources, and visit each other's rafts. You can also compete with other players to build the most impressive floating empire!
What kind of resources can I collect?
▼
You can collect various resources including wood, metal, plastic, and rare materials from the ocean. Each resource has its own uses in crafting and building, making resource management a key part of the game.
