Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start building?
▼
Simply select blocks from your palette and click to place them in the world. Use the various tools available to help you build faster and more efficiently!
Can I save my creations?
▼
Yes! You can save your builds and load them later. This allows you to work on multiple projects and share them with the community!
How do competitions work?
▼
In competitions, players are given a theme and time limit to build. After time's up, players vote on their favorite builds, and winners receive special rewards!
What are the different game modes?
▼
Bloxd.io features Creative mode for free building, Challenge mode for timed builds, Competition mode for build battles, and Multiplayer mode for collaborative building!
