Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control the power washer?
▼
Use your mouse to aim and click to spray water. You can adjust the pressure and angle for different cleaning needs. Move closer or further from surfaces to adjust the cleaning intensity.
Are there different cleaning tools available?
▼
Yes! You can unlock various nozzles and cleaning attachments as you progress through the game. Each tool is suited for different types of surfaces and cleaning challenges.
How do I track my progress?
▼
The game features a comprehensive progress tracking system that shows your completion percentage for each area, unlocked achievements, and overall cleaning statistics.
