Frequently Asked Questions

How do I interact with circus performers?
▼
Approach performers in the circus and press the interaction button to talk with them. Each character has unique dialogue and might offer special quests or teach you new abilities!
What are the different circus acts available?
▼
The circus features various acts including acrobatics, magic shows, clown performances, and spectacular digital illusions. Each act offers unique gameplay elements and rewards!
How do I unlock new areas of the circus?
▼
Complete performances, help circus performers, and collect special tickets to unlock new areas. Each area offers new challenges and exciting content to discover!
Are there special powers or abilities?
▼
Yes! As you progress through the game, you'll learn magical abilities from different performers. These powers help you solve puzzles, perform acts, and unlock new content!
