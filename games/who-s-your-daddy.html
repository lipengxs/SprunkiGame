About The Game

Who's Your Daddy? is a hilarious multiplayer game that pits a protective father against a mischievous baby in a battle of wits and reflexes. As the father, your mission is to baby-proof the house and prevent accidents, while as the baby, you're determined to find creative ways to get into trouble. With physics-based gameplay, countless interactive objects, and endless opportunities for chaos, every round brings new laughs and challenges. Play with friends and family to experience the comedic chaos of parenting gone wild!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play as the dad?
▼
As the dad, your goal is to protect the baby by baby-proofing the house, removing dangerous objects, and catching the baby before accidents happen. Use your tools and quick reflexes to prevent disasters.
What can I do as the baby?
▼
As the baby, you can crawl around the house, interact with objects, and find creative ways to get into mischief. Explore different rooms and discover various hazards while avoiding the watchful dad.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! The game is designed for multiplayer fun. One player takes the role of the dad while the other plays as the baby. You can also create custom games with your own rules.
Are there different maps?
▼
Yes, the game features various house layouts and rooms to explore. Each environment offers unique challenges and opportunities for both the dad and baby players.
