Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my creations?
▼
Click the save button in the top right corner. Your creation will be stored locally and can be accessed from the 'My Tracks' menu.
How do I unlock new sounds and characters?
▼
Complete challenges and achievements to unlock new content. Each character brings their own unique sound samples and effects!
Can I share my music with others?
▼
Yes! Use the export feature to save your creation as an audio file, which you can then share with friends or on social media.
What are corruption effects?
▼
Corruption effects are special filters that add unique twists to your sounds. Experiment with different combinations to create interesting musical effects!
