Frequently Asked Questions

What's different about human Sprunki?
▼
As a human, Sprunki has new abilities like running, jumping, and interacting with objects in different ways. This brings fresh gameplay mechanics and challenges to the classic Sprunki experience!
How do I unlock new abilities?
▼
Progress through the story and complete challenges to unlock new human abilities. Each new power helps you access new areas and solve more complex puzzles!
Are there different game modes?
▼
The game features a main story mode with side quests, challenge rooms, and collectible hunting. Each mode offers unique gameplay experiences!
What are the collectibles for?
▼
Collectibles unlock special content, enhance abilities, and reveal more about the story. Some also unlock secret areas and bonus challenges!
