Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play the game?
▼
Simply use your mouse or touch screen to interact with the virtual pimples. Click and drag to squeeze, and release at the right moment for maximum satisfaction and points!
Are there different game modes?
▼
Yes! The game features multiple modes including Time Trial, Challenge Mode, and Free Play. Each mode offers a unique way to experience the game.
How do I unlock new content?
▼
Progress through the game by completing levels and achieving high scores. New content, including special challenges and tools, will unlock as you earn achievements.
