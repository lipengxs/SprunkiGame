Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play with friends?
▼
You can easily join games with friends by inviting them to your party or joining the same server. The game supports multiplayer sessions with multiple players.
What maps are available?
▼
The game features various themed maps including mansions, schools, parks, and more. Each map offers unique hiding spots and challenges.
How do I earn rewards?
▼
You can earn rewards by winning matches, completing challenges, and participating in special events. These rewards can be used to unlock new characters and items.
Are there different game modes?
▼
Yes! The game features multiple modes including Classic, Sprint, Team Mode, and Competitive, each offering a unique twist on the hide and seek gameplay.
