Frequently Asked Questions

What's new in Phase 3?
▼
Phase 3 introduces new night-themed elements, enhanced graphics, more musical options, and improved multiplayer features. Players can expect a deeper, more immersive nocturnal experience.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! The Midnight Multiplayer mode allows you to create music with friends, sharing the nocturnal experience together in real-time.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes, the game offers multiple difficulty settings, from the relaxing Night Story mode to the challenging Starlight Mode, each with unique features and complexity levels.
How does the night cycle affect gameplay?
▼
The dynamic night cycle influences the music creation process, available sounds, and visual effects. Different phases of the moon unlock unique features and possibilities.
