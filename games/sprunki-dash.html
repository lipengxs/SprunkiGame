Frequently Asked Questions

How does the music system work?
▼
The music dynamically adapts to your gameplay! Your actions, movements, and collected power-ups all contribute to the musical composition, creating a unique soundtrack for each run.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! The multiplayer mode lets you race against friends or random players, competing for the highest score while creating collaborative musical experiences.
How do I unlock new worlds?
▼
New worlds are unlocked by completing challenges, achieving high scores, and collecting musical notes during your runs. Each world offers unique music styles and challenges!
Are there character upgrades?
▼
Yes! You can unlock and upgrade various abilities, customize your character's appearance, and enhance power-ups through the progression system.
