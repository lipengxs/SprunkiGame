Frequently Asked Questions

How do musical blocks work?
▼
Musical blocks produce sounds when placed and can be triggered by redstone or player interaction. Different blocks create different sounds, and their placement affects the overall composition.
Can I share my musical creations?
▼
Yes! You can share your musical worlds with other players, allowing them to explore and interact with your musical constructions. You can also collaborate with others in multiplayer mode.
How do I create custom sounds?
▼
The Block Crafter tool allows you to combine different sound elements and create custom musical blocks. You can also use the Sound Enchanter to add special effects to existing blocks.
What are musical biomes?
▼
Musical biomes are special areas in the game world where blocks produce themed sounds based on the environment. Each biome has its own unique musical characteristics and building possibilities.
