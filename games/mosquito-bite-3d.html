Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control the mosquito?
▼
Use WASD or arrow keys to fly around, and mouse to control direction. Space bar for quick altitude changes, and left click to attempt feeding when near a target. Master these controls to become an expert mosquito!
What are the main objectives?
▼
Your primary goal is survival - find food sources while avoiding dangers like swatters, sprays, and predators. Complete missions to unlock new areas and abilities, and try to build the largest mosquito colony!
Are there different game modes?
▼
Yes! Play in Story Mode to follow the mosquito's journey, Survival Mode for endless challenges, or Mission Mode to complete specific objectives. Each mode offers unique gameplay experiences!
