Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start building?
▼
Select your building tools from the inventory, choose your blocks or materials, and start placing them in the 3D world. Use the various tools to rotate, scale, and position your creations exactly how you want them!
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! Sprunki Craft - Sandbox 3D supports multiplayer gameplay. Join existing worlds or create your own and invite friends to build and explore together!
How do I save my creations?
▼
Your creations are automatically saved as you build. You can also create blueprints of your builds to share with others or use them in different worlds!
