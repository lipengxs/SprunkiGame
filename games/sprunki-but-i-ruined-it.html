Frequently Asked Questions

Is the game actually broken?
▼
No! The game is intentionally designed to look and sound "broken" while being perfectly playable. It's a creative take on glitch art and experimental music!
Can I save my glitched creations?
▼
Absolutely! You can save, share, and export your chaotic compositions - if the save system isn't "accidentally" corrupted (it's not, we promise).
How do I make good music if everything is broken?
▼
That's the beauty of it! The "broken" elements are carefully designed to create interesting and unique sounds. Embrace the chaos, and you might create something amazing!
Are there any actual bugs?
▼
While the game appears glitchy and broken, it's all intentional! The actual gameplay mechanics work perfectly - they just create wonderfully chaotic results.
