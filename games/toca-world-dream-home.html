Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock new furniture?
▼
New furniture items can be unlocked by completing design challenges, earning points, and progressing through the game. Some special items are available during seasonal events.
Can I save multiple designs?
▼
Yes! You can save multiple design projects and work on them at your own pace. The game automatically saves your progress, and you can create different versions of the same room.
How do I share my designs?
▼
You can share your designs with the community through the built-in sharing feature. Take screenshots of your creations and inspire others with your unique style.
Are there design templates?
▼
Yes, the game offers various room templates and style themes to help you get started. Use these as inspiration or as a foundation for your own creative designs.
