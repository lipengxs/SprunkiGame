Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start farming?
▼
Begin with basic crops and gradually expand your farm. The game includes helpful tutorials to guide you through the basics!
What animals can I raise?
▼
You can raise various animals including chickens, cows, pigs, and more. Each animal provides different products and requires unique care.
Are there seasons?
▼
Yes! The game features a seasonal system where different crops grow better in different seasons, adding depth to farming strategy.
