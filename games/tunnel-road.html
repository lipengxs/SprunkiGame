Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control my vehicle?
▼
Use arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle's movement. Left and right for steering, up for boost (when available), and down for brake. Quick reflexes and precise timing are key to achieving high scores!
What are the different power-ups?
▼
Power-ups include speed boosts, shields for protection, score multipliers, and special abilities. Each power-up has a unique effect that can help you survive longer and achieve higher scores.
How do I unlock new vehicles?
▼
New vehicles can be unlocked by reaching specific score milestones or completing special challenges. Each vehicle has unique characteristics like speed, handling, and special abilities!
