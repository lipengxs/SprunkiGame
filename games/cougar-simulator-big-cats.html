Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hunt effectively in the game?
▼
Use stealth mode to approach prey quietly, observe their behavior patterns, and strike when they're vulnerable. Different prey requires different hunting strategies.
Can I customize my cougar?
▼
Yes! You can customize your cougar's appearance with different fur patterns and physical attributes.
How do I take care of cubs?
▼
Cubs need regular feeding, protection from predators, and training in hunting skills. Keep them safe in your den and gradually teach them survival techniques.
