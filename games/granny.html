About The Game

Granny is a terrifying horror survival game that puts you in a nightmarish situation. Trapped in a mysterious house, you must find a way to escape while avoiding the deadly Granny who's constantly hunting you. Use stealth, solve puzzles, and gather items needed for your escape, but be careful - one wrong move could be your last.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the game too scary?
▼
Granny is designed to be an intense horror experience. While it contains jump scares and tense moments, players can adjust to their comfort level by choosing different difficulty settings.
How do I escape the house?
▼
To escape, you'll need to find various items scattered throughout the house while avoiding Granny. These items can be used to unlock doors or create tools for your escape.
Are there multiple endings?
▼
Yes! The game features multiple endings based on how you escape and what you discover during your playthrough.
What makes this game unique?
▼
Granny combines intense horror elements with strategic gameplay and puzzle-solving. The advanced AI system makes each playthrough unique and unpredictable.
