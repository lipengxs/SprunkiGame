My Land: King Defender is an engaging strategy defense game where you must protect your kingdom from waves of invading enemies. Build and upgrade defensive structures, manage resources, and command troops to ensure your kingdom's survival in this epic medieval adventure.

How do I start defending my kingdom?
▼

▼
