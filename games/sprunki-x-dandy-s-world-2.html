Frequently Asked Questions

What's new in this sequel?
▼
Sprunki x Dandy's World 2 introduces new characters, enhanced music creation tools, expanded worlds to explore, and improved collaborative features for playing with friends.
Can I play without playing the first game?
▼
Yes! While fans of the original will appreciate the references, the game is designed to be enjoyable for newcomers with an intuitive tutorial system.
How does the music creation work?
▼
The game features an intuitive music creation system where you can combine different sounds, beats, and melodies from both universes to create unique musical compositions.
Are there multiplayer features?
▼
Yes, you can collaborate with friends to create music together, share your creations, and participate in musical challenges with other players.
