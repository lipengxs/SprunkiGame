Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start digging?
▼
Begin with the basic shovel tool and click to dig. As you progress, you'll unlock more efficient tools and techniques. Pay attention to the tutorial for essential digging mechanics and tips.
What can I find while digging?
▼
You can discover various treasures including rare minerals, ancient artifacts, hidden passages, and special items. Each discovery helps you progress and unlock new areas to explore.
How do I upgrade my tools?
▼
Collect resources while digging and use them at the upgrade station to improve your tools. Better tools allow you to dig faster and access new areas of the underground world.
