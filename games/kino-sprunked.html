Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Kino Sprunked unique?
▼
Kino Sprunked combines professional orchestral instruments with cinematic scenes, allowing you to create film-quality scores while seeing your music come to life visually.
Can I export my compositions?
▼
Yes! You can export your compositions as audio files, and even save them with their synchronized visual scenes for sharing or further editing.
Do I need musical experience?
▼
While musical knowledge can be helpful, our intuitive interface and tutorial system make it easy for beginners to create impressive cinematic scores.
How does the collaboration mode work?
▼
Collaboration mode allows multiple composers to work on the same project in real-time, with features for communication and role assignment.
