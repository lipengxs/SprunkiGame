About The Game

Welcome to TABS: Epic Battle Simulator, where strategy meets chaos in spectacular physics-based warfare. Command diverse armies of unique units, position them strategically on the battlefield, and watch as hilarious and epic battles unfold. With ragdoll physics, varied unit types, and multiple battle scenarios, every fight is unpredictable and entertaining. Create your own battle strategies, experiment with different unit combinations, and lead your forces to victory in this engaging battle simulator.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock new units?
▼
New units can be unlocked by completing campaign missions, winning challenges, and earning victory points. Some special units are unlocked through specific achievements.
What's the best strategy for winning battles?
▼
Success depends on unit placement, terrain utilization, and army composition. Try to counter enemy units and use the environment to your advantage.
Can I create custom battles?
▼
Yes! In Sandbox mode, you can create custom battles by choosing your units, enemy forces, and battle environment. Experiment with different combinations for endless fun.
Are there regular updates?
▼
Yes, the game receives regular updates with new units, maps, battle scenarios, and gameplay improvements based on player feedback.
