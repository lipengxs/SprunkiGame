About The Game

Prison Break is an intense strategy adventure game that challenges you to orchestrate the perfect escape. Use stealth, wit, and careful planning to navigate through a high-security prison. Collect items, solve puzzles, and avoid guards as you work your way to freedom.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I avoid guards?
▼
Study guard patrol patterns, use stealth mechanics, and time your movements carefully. You can also create distractions to slip past unnoticed.
What items can I craft?
▼
Collect materials to craft various tools like lockpicks, disguises, and other items essential for your escape plan.
Are there multiple endings?
▼
Yes! Your choices and the escape route you take can lead to different outcomes. Try different strategies to discover all possible endings!
