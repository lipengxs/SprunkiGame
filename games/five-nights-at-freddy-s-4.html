About The Game

In the fourth chapter of Five Nights at Freddy's, the horror has finally reached where you live. As a child whose role is yet to be determined, you must defend yourself against Freddy Fazbear, Chica, Bonnie, Foxy, and even worse things that lurk in the shadows. Playing with only a flashlight and doors, you must survive until 6am by watching the corridors, behind you, and your closet.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I survive the nights?
▼
Listen carefully for audio cues, manage your flashlight power wisely, and check all possible entry points regularly. Remember to close doors when you hear breathing and use your flashlight to ward off animatronics in the hallways.
What's new in FNAF 4?
▼
FNAF 4 introduces a new home setting, more terrifying nightmare versions of the classic animatronics, and new gameplay mechanics including listening for breathing and managing multiple entry points in your bedroom.
Is it scarier than previous games?
▼
Many consider FNAF 4 to be the scariest in the series due to its intimate home setting, more aggressive animatronics, and enhanced jump scares. The psychological horror elements are also more pronounced.
Are there multiple endings?
▼
Yes, the game features multiple endings based on your performance and discoveries throughout the nights. Each ending reveals different aspects of the game's mysterious story.
