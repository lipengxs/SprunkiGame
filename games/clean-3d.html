Frequently Asked Questions

How do I solve 3D puzzles?
▼
Experiment with different perspectives, manipulate objects in 3D space, and think about how elements interact. Sometimes the solution requires viewing the puzzle from multiple angles!
Are there hidden secrets?
▼
Yes! Each level contains hidden paths and secrets that can be discovered by exploring thoroughly and thinking creatively about the 3D space.
How do I progress through levels?
▼
Complete each puzzle to unlock new levels. The difficulty increases gradually, introducing new mechanics and challenges as you progress through the game!
