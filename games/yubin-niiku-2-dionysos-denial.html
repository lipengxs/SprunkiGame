About The Game

Yubin Niiku 2: Dionysos' Denial is an enchanting RPG adventure that seamlessly blends Japanese and Greek mythology. In this epic sequel, players embark on a journey through mystical realms to uncover the truth behind Dionysos' mysterious denial of the ancient pact. With enhanced combat mechanics, deeper character customization, and a rich narrative, this game offers an unforgettable gaming experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to play the first game to understand the story?
▼
While playing the first game enhances the experience, Yubin Niiku 2 includes a comprehensive story recap and can be enjoyed as a standalone adventure!
How long is the main story?
▼
The main story takes approximately 20-25 hours to complete, with additional side quests and content that can extend gameplay to 40+ hours.
Are there different character classes?
▼
Yes! You can choose from multiple character classes, each with unique abilities and skill trees that can be customized to your playstyle.
