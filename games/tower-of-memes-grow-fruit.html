Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock new memes?
▼
New memes are unlocked by progressing through levels, completing challenges, and discovering special fruit combinations. Keep growing and experimenting to find them all!
What are the best strategies for tower building?
▼
Focus on balancing your fruit types, upgrading your growing spaces, and utilizing meme combinations effectively. Start with common fruits and gradually work your way up to rare and legendary varieties.
How often are new fruits added?
▼
New fruits and memes are added regularly through updates, usually monthly. Special event fruits are also introduced during seasonal events and holidays.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can compete with friends in various challenges, share your tower progress, and even trade certain fruit varieties. Join the community to enhance your gaming experience!
