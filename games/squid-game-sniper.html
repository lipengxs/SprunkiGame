Frequently Asked Questions

How do I improve my accuracy in the game?
▼
Practice in Training Mode to master the basics of aiming and shooting. Pay attention to wind direction and distance, and upgrade your weapons and scopes to improve accuracy.
What types of missions are available?
▼
The game features various mission types including story missions, time trials, target practice, and endless survival mode. Each mission type offers unique challenges and rewards.
How do I unlock new weapons?
▼
New weapons can be unlocked by completing missions, earning achievements, and progressing through the campaign mode. Each weapon has unique characteristics and can be further customized.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes, the game offers multiple difficulty levels from Rookie to Expert. Higher difficulties feature more challenging conditions like stronger wind effects and moving targets.
