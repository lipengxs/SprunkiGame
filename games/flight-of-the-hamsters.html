Frequently Asked Questions

How do I improve my flight distance?
▼
Focus on timing your launch perfectly, collect power-ups during flight, and upgrade your equipment between attempts. The better your timing and equipment, the further you'll fly!
What upgrades are available?
▼
You can upgrade your launch pad for better initial boost, improve your glider for better air control, enhance your propeller for extra thrust, and collect various power-ups that help you fly further!
How do I unlock new hamsters?
▼
New hamster characters can be unlocked by achieving specific distances, collecting special items, or completing daily challenges. Each hamster has unique abilities!
What are the daily challenges?
▼
Daily challenges offer special missions with unique objectives and rewards. Complete them to earn bonus coins, unlock special items, and climb the leaderboard!
