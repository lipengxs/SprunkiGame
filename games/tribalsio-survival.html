Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my own tribe?
▼
Starting a tribe is easy! Begin by gathering resources and building a base. Once you've established yourself, you can invite other players to join your tribe and start expanding your territory.
Is the game free to play?
▼
Yes! Tribals.io Survival is completely free to play. Jump in and start your tribal adventure without any cost.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Absolutely! You can team up with friends by forming a tribe together. Coordinate your strategies and work together to become the most powerful tribe in the game.
What makes this game unique?
▼
Tribals.io Survival combines classic IO game mechanics with deep survival and tribal warfare elements. The unique blend of building, resource management, and multiplayer combat creates an engaging experience unlike any other.
