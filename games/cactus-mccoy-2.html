Frequently Asked Questions

What's new in Cactus McCoy 2?
▼
Cactus McCoy 2 features enhanced combat mechanics, new weapons and abilities, larger territories to explore, and more challenging enemies than its predecessor.
How do I upgrade my character?
▼
Collect treasures and defeat enemies to earn upgrade points, which can be used to enhance McCoy's abilities, weapons, and stats.
Are there different game modes?
▼
Yes! The game features Story Mode, Challenge Mode, Endless Mode, and Achievement Hunt, each offering unique gameplay experiences.
How do I find hidden treasures?
▼
Explore thoroughly, look for secret passages, and solve environmental puzzles to discover hidden treasures throughout the game world.
