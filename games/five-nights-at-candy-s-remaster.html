About The Game

Five Nights at Candy's Remaster brings the classic horror survival game to life with enhanced graphics and gameplay mechanics. Take on the role of a night security guard at Candy's restaurant, where you must survive your shift by monitoring security cameras, managing power resources, and keeping the animatronics at bay. Frequently Asked Questions

How do I survive the night?
▼
Monitor the security cameras, manage your power usage wisely, and keep track of the animatronics' movements. Close doors when necessary, but remember that each action consumes power. You need to find the right balance to make it until 6 AM.
What's new in the remaster?
▼
The remastered version features improved graphics, enhanced animations, better sound design, and refined gameplay mechanics. You'll also find new easter eggs and secrets to discover throughout the game.
How many nights are there?
▼
The game features multiple nights, each becoming progressively more difficult. The animatronics become more aggressive and active as you progress, making each night a unique challenge to overcome.
