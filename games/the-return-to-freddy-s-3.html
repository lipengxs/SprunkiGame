About The Game

The Return to Freddy's 3 brings the horror of the night shift to a whole new level. As the security guard at Freddy's, you must survive through the night while managing limited power resources and keeping a watchful eye on the haunted animatronics. With enhanced AI, new mechanics, and even more terrifying encounters, this third installment pushes the boundaries of survival horror.
