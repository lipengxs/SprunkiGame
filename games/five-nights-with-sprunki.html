About The Game

Five Nights with Sprunki combines survival horror with rhythm gameplay in a unique musical nightmare. As the night security guard at an abandoned music studio, you must survive five nights while managing your resources and creating protective beats. Watch the security cameras, listen for approaching threats, and use your musical abilities to defend yourself!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I survive the night?
▼
Monitor the security cameras, listen for audio cues, and create defensive beats when threats approach. Managing your power usage is crucial - you need to last until 6 AM!
What are defensive beats?
▼
Defensive beats are rhythmic patterns you create that can repel or deter threats. Different threats require different beat patterns, so learn and adapt your strategies each night.
Does each night get harder?
▼
Yes! Each night introduces new challenges, threats, and mechanics. The difficulty progressively increases, with Night Five being the ultimate test of survival.
Are there secret endings?
▼
The game features multiple endings based on your performance and discoveries throughout the five nights. Keep exploring to uncover all the secrets!
