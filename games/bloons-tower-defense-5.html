Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing?
▼
Choose a map and difficulty level, then start placing towers strategically to pop the incoming balloons. The game includes helpful tutorials!
What towers are available?
▼
There are many tower types including Dart Monkeys, Tack Shooters, Super Monkeys, and more. Each has unique abilities and upgrade paths!
How do I unlock new content?
▼
Progress through the game to earn experience and money, which can be used to unlock new towers, upgrades, and special agents.
