Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started in Combat Online?
▼
Begin with the tutorial to learn basic controls and mechanics. Start with deathmatch mode to practice your skills before joining team-based matches.
What are the different character classes?
▼
Combat Online features multiple classes including Assault, Support, Sniper, and Heavy. Each class has unique abilities and weapon preferences.
How does the ranking system work?
▼
Players earn ranking points based on match performance. Win matches and complete objectives to climb the ranks and face tougher opponents.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can team up with friends in party mode or join the same team in team-based matches. Coordinate strategies for better chances of victory.
