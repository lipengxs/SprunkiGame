Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Angry Birds?
▼
Pull back on the slingshot to launch birds at the pig's structures. Aim carefully and use each bird's special ability by tapping while in flight. Try to destroy all pigs with as few birds as possible to earn three stars!
What are the different types of birds?
▼
Each bird has unique abilities: Red Bird is basic, Yellow Bird speeds up mid-flight, Blue Bird splits into three, Black Bird explodes, White Bird drops egg bombs, and more to discover!
How do I get three stars?
▼
To earn three stars, you need to complete the level efficiently by using fewer birds and causing maximum destruction. Try different angles and strategies to improve your score!
Are there power-ups available?
▼
Yes! You can unlock various power-ups as you progress through the game. These include the Mighty Eagle and other special abilities to help you tackle challenging levels.
