About The Game

Welcome to Granny's Classroom Nightmare, a heart-pounding horror adventure that will test your survival skills and nerve. Search for clues, solve puzzles, and find a way to escape before it's too late. With multiple possible endings and increasing difficulty levels, every playthrough brings new challenges and scares!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I survive longer?
▼
Stay quiet, hide in strategic locations, and always keep an eye on your surroundings. Learning granny's movement patterns and timing your actions are crucial for survival!
Are there different endings?
▼
Yes! The game features multiple endings based on your choices, items collected, and the escape route you choose. Try different strategies to discover all possible outcomes!
How do I solve the puzzles?
▼
Pay attention to classroom details, collect items, and look for clues in your surroundings. Each puzzle has logical solutions, but you'll need to think creatively and act quickly!
