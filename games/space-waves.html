Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control my spacecraft?
▼
Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your spacecraft. The up and down keys control your altitude, while left and right keys help you dodge obstacles and collect power-ups.
What are cosmic waves?
▼
Cosmic waves are energy patterns that appear throughout the game. Riding these waves correctly will boost your speed and score multiplier, making them essential for achieving high scores!
How do I earn achievements?
▼
Achievements are unlocked by completing specific challenges, such as reaching distance milestones, collecting certain power-ups, or maintaining perfect wave rides for extended periods.
