Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my zoo in Zoo 2: Animal Park?
▼
Begin with the tutorial that guides you through basic zoo management, including building your first habitat and caring for your initial animals. You'll learn essential mechanics while developing your park step by step.
What types of animals can I have in my zoo?
▼
Zoo 2: Animal Park features a wide variety of animals, from exotic big cats to cute pandas, tropical birds, and aquatic creatures. Each species has unique care requirements and habitat needs.
How do I make my zoo profitable?
▼
Focus on visitor satisfaction by providing diverse attractions, well-maintained habitats, and good facilities. Balance ticket prices, manage operating costs, and invest in popular exhibits to maximize profits.
Are there special events in the game?
▼
Yes! The game features regular special events, seasonal celebrations, and limited-time challenges that offer unique rewards and new animals for your zoo.
