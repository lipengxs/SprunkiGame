Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with the puzzles?
▼
Begin with the tutorial levels that introduce basic concepts and mechanics. Each puzzle category has its own tutorial section to help you understand the specific rules and objectives. As you progress, the difficulty will gradually increase.
What types of puzzles are available?
▼
The game features a wide variety of puzzle types including pattern matching, logic puzzles, spatial reasoning challenges, number puzzles, and word games. Each category offers unique challenges and requires different problem-solving approaches.
Is there a hint system if I get stuck?
▼
Yes! The game includes a smart hint system that provides progressive clues without giving away the complete solution. You can use hints strategically to overcome particularly challenging puzzles while still maintaining the satisfaction of solving them yourself.
