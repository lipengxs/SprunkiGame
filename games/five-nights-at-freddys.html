About The Game

Five Nights at Freddy's puts you in the role of a night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, where you must survive five nights while managing limited power resources and watching suspicious animatronic characters through security cameras. This intense horror survival game will test your nerves and strategic thinking.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I survive the night?
▼
Monitor the cameras carefully, manage your power usage, and use doors and lights strategically to keep the animatronics out!
What happens if I run out of power?
▼
When power runs out, you become vulnerable to the animatronics. Try to conserve power by using cameras and doors sparingly.
Are there multiple endings?
▼
Yes! The game features different endings based on your performance and discoveries throughout the nights.
