Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt my first pet?
▼
Visit the adoption center in the game to choose your first pet. You'll find various pets available, each with their own unique characteristics and needs.
Can I have multiple pets?
▼
Yes! You can adopt and care for multiple pets. Each pet requires attention and care, so make sure you can manage them all.
How does pet trading work?
▼
You can trade pets with other players in designated trading areas. Make sure to review trades carefully and only accept trades you're comfortable with.
