Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my space journey?
▼
Begin with the tutorial mission that teaches you basic space navigation and exploration mechanics. Learn to control your spacecraft and use essential tools before venturing into deep space!
Can I customize my spacecraft?
▼
Yes! You can upgrade and customize your spacecraft with various modules, weapons, and equipment. Collect resources and trade with alien species to unlock new customization options.
What can I do on different planets?
▼
Each planet offers unique opportunities for exploration, resource gathering, and interaction with alien life. You can establish colonies, conduct research, trade with locals, and uncover ancient artifacts and mysteries!
