Frequently Asked Questions

How similar is this to the TV show?
▼
The game closely follows the format of the TV show, featuring 26 briefcases, banker offers, and the same exciting decision-making moments that made the show famous.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! The multiplayer mode allows you to compete with friends, comparing your decision-making skills and seeing who can win the biggest prizes.
How are banker offers calculated?
▼
The banker uses a sophisticated AI algorithm that considers remaining values, number of cases left, and your playing style to make realistic offers, just like in the TV show.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes, you can choose from different difficulty settings that affect banker behavior and prize distributions, making the game more challenging as you progress.
