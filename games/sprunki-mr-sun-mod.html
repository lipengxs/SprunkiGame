Progress through the Daybreak Mode and complete challenges to gradually unlock all sun powers and special abilities.

▼
Yes! The mod includes a variety of new sun-themed sound effects and musical elements that create a bright and uplifting atmosphere.

Yes, you can still access the original Sprunki modes while enjoying the new sun-themed content and features.
