About The Game

Slither.io is an addictive multiplayer snake game where you compete against players from around the world. Control your snake, consume glowing orbs to grow longer, and try to outmaneuver other players. Be careful though - crashing into other snakes means game over! With simple controls but deep strategy, every game is a unique challenge as you try to climb the leaderboard and become the biggest snake in the arena.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control my snake?
▼
Move your mouse to control your snake's direction. Hold the left mouse button or spacebar to activate speed boost, but be careful as this will make you lose mass faster.
How do I grow bigger?
▼
Collect glowing orbs scattered around the arena or consume the remains of defeated snakes. The more you eat, the longer and bigger your snake becomes.
How do I get new skins?
▼
New skins can be unlocked by achieving certain milestones or participating in special events. Some skins are also available through the customization menu.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can play with friends in Party Mode by sharing your server code, or team up in Team Mode to work together against other players.
