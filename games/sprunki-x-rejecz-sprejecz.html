Frequently Asked Questions

How does the fusion system work?
▼
The fusion system allows you to seamlessly blend elements from both Sprunki and Rejecz, creating unique combinations through various mixing tools and style morphing options.
Can I use existing Sprunki or Rejecz content?
▼
Yes! The game includes content from both universes, and you can import your existing creations from either platform to remix and blend them in new ways.
Is online collaboration available?
▼
Absolutely! The Collab Mode lets you work with other creators in real-time, combining different styles and approaches to create unique fusion tracks.
What makes this different from regular Sprunki games?
▼
This unique collaboration brings together two distinct musical worlds, offering new sounds, tools, and possibilities that aren't available in either platform alone. It's a completely new way to create and experience music!
