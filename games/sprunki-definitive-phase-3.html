Frequently Asked Questions

What's new in the Definitive Edition?
▼
The Definitive Edition includes enhanced graphics, improved sound processing, refined controls, expanded content library, and numerous quality-of-life improvements based on community feedback.
Can I import my old projects?
▼
Yes! The Definitive Edition is fully compatible with original Phase 3 projects. Your existing creations will automatically benefit from the enhanced features and improved sound quality.
What are the new creation tools?
▼
New tools include an advanced pattern editor, expanded effect suite, improved mixing capabilities, and a refined project management system. These tools offer more precise control over your musical creations.
Is it suitable for beginners?
▼
Absolutely! While the Definitive Edition adds advanced features, it maintains an intuitive interface with improved tutorials and guidance for newcomers. Start with Classic mode and gradually explore new features at your own pace.
