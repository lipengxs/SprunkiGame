Frequently Asked Questions

Is this game suitable for children?
▼
Yes! Sprunki Mr.Tree Family is designed to be family-friendly and educational, perfect for players of all ages to enjoy together.
How do family members interact in the game?
▼
Each family member has unique musical abilities that combine to create beautiful harmonies. Players can switch between family members or play together in multiplayer mode.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes, the game features progressive difficulty levels suitable for both beginners and experienced players, making it perfect for family play sessions.
Can we save our family's musical creations?
▼
Absolutely! You can save your family's musical compositions and special moments in the Family Memory Album to revisit and share them later.
