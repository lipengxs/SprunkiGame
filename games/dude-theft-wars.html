Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing Dude Theft Wars?
▼
Simply click the Play Now button and start exploring the open world. The game includes a tutorial that will guide you through the basic mechanics and controls.
What types of vehicles can I steal?
▼
The game features a wide variety of vehicles including cars, motorcycles, trucks, and even special vehicles. Each vehicle has its own handling characteristics and performance stats.
How do I make money in the game?
▼
You can earn money through various activities including completing missions, selling stolen vehicles, robbing stores, and completing side jobs. The more risky the activity, the higher the potential reward.
Can I customize my character?
▼
Yes, the game features extensive character customization options. You can change your character's appearance, clothes, and accessories by visiting in-game shops and using the money you've earned.
