About The Game

Papa Louie 2: When Burgers Attack! is a delicious platformer adventure where evil food has come to life! As Papa Louie and his loyal customers, you must battle through unique worlds filled with mischievous burgers, rescue trapped customers, and restore peace to the Restaurant Kingdom. Use special abilities, collect ingredients, and serve up justice in this tasty adventure!
