Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control my character?
▼
Use the spacebar or mouse click to jump, fly, and interact with the game. Timing is crucial - sync your actions with the music for the best results!
Can I create my own levels?
▼
Yes! The game includes a level editor where you can design and share your own custom levels with the community. Let your creativity flow!
Is there a practice mode?
▼
Yes, there's a practice mode where you can learn the mechanics and perfect your timing without the pressure of completing the entire level.
