Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control traffic?
▼
You'll manage traffic lights, direct vehicles, and make strategic decisions to keep traffic flowing smoothly. The game features intuitive controls and a helpful tutorial to get you started.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes! The game features progressive difficulty across different levels and modes, from basic intersections to complex multi-road challenges that will test your traffic management skills.
What types of vehicles are there?
▼
The game includes a variety of vehicles including cars, buses, trucks, and emergency vehicles, each with their own characteristics and priorities in traffic flow.
How do I earn achievements?
▼
Achievements can be earned by completing specific challenges, maintaining smooth traffic flow, preventing accidents, and mastering different game modes and scenarios.
