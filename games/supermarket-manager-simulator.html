Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my supermarket?
▼
Begin with the tutorial mode to learn the basics of store management, including inventory control, staff hiring, and customer service. Then progress to career mode to build your retail empire.
What management features are available?
▼
The game includes comprehensive management features such as inventory control, staff management, financial planning, store layout optimization, and customer relationship management.
Can I expand my business?
▼
Yes! As you progress and earn profits, you can expand your store, add new departments, upgrade equipment, and even open additional locations.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
The game offers multiple difficulty levels, from beginner to expert, each with unique challenges and management complexities to test your skills.
