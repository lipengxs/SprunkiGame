Frequently Asked Questions

How do I build effective vehicles?
▼
Start with a strong frame and add wheels for ground movement. For flying vehicles, balance is key - use symmetrical designs and multiple propulsion sources.
What's the best way to collect stars?
▼
Stars are usually placed in challenging positions. Build vehicles specifically designed for each level's layout and try different approaches to reach them all.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes, levels progressively get more challenging, introducing new mechanics and requiring more complex contraptions. The sandbox mode is great for practice.
How do I unlock new parts?
▼
New parts are unlocked by progressing through levels and completing specific challenges. Some special parts can be earned through achievements.
