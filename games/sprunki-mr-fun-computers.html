Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need music production experience?
▼
Not at all! The retro-inspired interface is designed to be intuitive and fun for both beginners and experienced producers. Start with simple patterns and explore more complex features as you learn.
Can I export my creations?
▼
Yes! You can export your music in various formats, including WAV, MP3, and even vintage formats for authentic retro feel.
What kind of sounds are available?
▼
The game features a wide range of sounds from different computer eras, from classic 8-bit chiptunes to modern digital synthesis, all authentically recreated for maximum nostalgia!
Is MIDI support included?
▼
Yes! You can connect MIDI controllers for hands-on control of the vintage-inspired synthesizers and sequencers.
