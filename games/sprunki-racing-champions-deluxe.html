Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock new vehicles and parts?
▼
Progress through the career mode and earn credits by winning races. You can use these credits to purchase new vehicles and parts. Some special vehicles and parts can be unlocked by completing specific challenges or participating in limited-time events.
What race modes are available?
▼
The game features various race modes including Circuit Racing, Time Trial, Drift Competition, Drag Racing, and Endurance Events. Each mode has its own unique challenges and rewards. You can also participate in weekly tournaments and special events.
Can I race against my friends?
▼
Absolutely! You can create private lobbies to race against friends, join public multiplayer races, or compete in team-based events. The game also features a comprehensive ranking system and global leaderboards to track your progress.
