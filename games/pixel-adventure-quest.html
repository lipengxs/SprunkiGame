Frequently Asked Questions

What are the controls?
▼
Use arrow keys or WASD to move, SPACE to jump, and Z or X for special abilities. The controls can be customized in the options menu.
How do I save my progress?
▼
The game automatically saves your progress after completing each level and collecting achievements.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes! You can choose between Easy, Normal, and Hard difficulty modes, each offering different challenges and rewards.
