Frequently Asked Questions

How do I perform special ninja moves?
▼
Use combinations of arrow keys and space bar to execute special moves. Double-tap directional keys for dash moves, and hold space while running on walls for wall-running!
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes! The game offers three difficulty levels: Novice, Warrior, and Master Ninja. Each level affects enemy awareness, damage taken, and checkpoint frequency.
Can I customize my ninja character?
▼
Absolutely! As you progress through the game, you'll unlock various ninja outfits, weapons, and special abilities that you can customize to match your playstyle.
