Frequently Asked Questions

What's new in MSI Phase 3?
▼
MSI Phase 3 introduces enhanced sound quality, new instruments, improved mixing capabilities, and a more intuitive user interface. Players can now create more complex musical arrangements and explore new creative possibilities.
How do I save my compositions?
▼
Your compositions are automatically saved as you create them. You can access your saved works from the main menu and share them with other players through the built-in sharing system.
Is this game suitable for beginners?
▼
Yes! While MSI Phase 3 offers advanced features for experienced users, it includes an intuitive tutorial system that helps beginners learn the basics of music creation and rhythm gameplay.
