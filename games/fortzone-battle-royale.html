Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get better at building?
▼
Practice building in the creative mode first. Learn basic patterns like walls and ramps, then combine them into more complex structures. Speed and efficiency come with practice.
What's the best strategy for winning?
▼
Balance between aggressive and defensive play. Land in less populated areas to gear up, gather resources for building, and position yourself strategically as the storm closes in.
How does the storm system work?
▼
The storm circle shrinks periodically, forcing players closer together. Stay inside the safe zone to avoid taking damage, and plan your movements ahead of time.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can team up with friends in Duos or Squads mode. Communication and coordination are key to success in team modes.
