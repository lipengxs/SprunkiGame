About The Game

Welcome back to the new and improved Freddy Fazbear's Pizza! In Five Nights at Freddy's 2, you must survive even more terrifying nights as a security guard, facing new animatronic threats while managing limited resources. With no doors to protect you, you'll need to rely on your wits, a flashlight, and an empty Freddy Fazbear head to survive until 6 AM.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I survive the night?
▼
Monitor the security cameras, keep the music box wound up, use your flashlight wisely, and wear the Freddy mask when animatronics enter your office. Each animatronic has different patterns and behaviors you'll need to learn.
What's new in Five Nights at Freddy's 2?
▼
The sequel introduces new toy animatronics, removes the security doors, adds the Freddy mask mechanic, and includes a music box that must be kept wound up. There's also new customization options in Custom Night mode.
How do I use the Freddy mask?
▼
When an animatronic enters your office, quickly put on the Freddy mask to fool them into thinking you're another animatronic. However, some animatronics like Foxy and The Puppet aren't fooled by the mask.
What is Custom Night mode?
▼
Custom Night allows you to adjust the AI difficulty of each animatronic from 0 to 20, creating your own custom challenge. There are also preset challenges with special rewards for completion.
