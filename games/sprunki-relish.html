Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need musical experience to play?
▼
No musical experience is required! Sprunki Relish is designed to be accessible to everyone, with intuitive controls and helpful tutorials to guide you through the basics of music creation.
Can I save my creations?
▼
Yes! You can save all your musical creations and access them anytime. You can also share them with friends and the Sprunki community.
How does multiplayer work?
▼
Multiplayer mode allows you to collaborate with friends in real-time, creating music together and sharing ideas. You can join existing sessions or create your own.
Are there regular updates?
▼
Yes! We regularly update Sprunki Relish with new instruments, sounds, features, and community-requested content to keep the experience fresh and exciting.
