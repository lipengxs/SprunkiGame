About The Game

Sprunki Scratch is an innovative fusion of music creation and visual programming. Learn to code while making music, or make music while learning to code! Use block-based programming to create beats, compose melodies, and craft unique musical algorithms in this educational yet entertaining experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need programming experience?
▼
Not at all! Sprunki Scratch is designed for beginners with an intuitive block-based interface. Our interactive tutorials will guide you through everything you need to know.
Can I share my musical code?
▼
Yes! You can share your creations with the community, remix others' projects, and collaborate with fellow creators.
What programming concepts will I learn?
▼
You'll learn fundamental programming concepts like loops, variables, functions, and conditional logic - all through the fun process of creating music!
Can I import my own sounds?
▼
Yes! Advanced users can import their own sound samples to use in their musical programming projects.
