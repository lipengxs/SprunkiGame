Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing?
▼
Simply click 'Play Now' to join a game. You'll be matched with other players and begin with the first challenge. Remember to read the rules of each game carefully before starting.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can invite friends to join the same game session. While you may start as allies, remember that only one player can ultimately win the game.
What happens if I lose?
▼
If you're eliminated, you can immediately join a new game or spectate the current one. Your progress and any earned rewards are saved to your profile.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
The game automatically matches you with players of similar skill levels. You can also practice in training mode to improve your skills before joining competitive matches.
