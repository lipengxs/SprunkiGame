Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock new recipes?
▼
New recipes can be unlocked by completing challenges, reaching higher levels, and satisfying customer requests. Some special recipes are unlocked through in-game events.
Can I customize my restaurant?
▼
Yes! You can customize your restaurant's layout, decoration, and equipment. Unlock new items as you progress and create your dream restaurant.
How does the rating system work?
▼
Your restaurant's rating is based on food quality, service speed, customer satisfaction, and restaurant ambiance. Higher ratings attract more customers and unlock special features.
Are there regular updates?
▼
Yes, the game receives regular updates with new recipes, equipment, seasonal events, and gameplay improvements based on player feedback.
