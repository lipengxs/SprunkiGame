About The Game

In Haunt the House, you play as a playful ghost with the power to possess and animate various objects throughout your house. Your goal is to scare away all the unwanted inhabitants while having fun with your ghostly abilities. Explore different rooms, discover unique objects to possess, and create increasingly spooky scenarios to achieve your goal.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start haunting?
▼
Simply approach any interactive object and press the interaction button to possess it. Each object has unique abilities and ways to scare the inhabitants. Experiment with different objects to find the most effective scare tactics.
What objects can I possess?
▼
You can possess a wide variety of objects throughout the house, from furniture and appliances to decorations and toys. Each object has its own unique animations and scare potential.
How do I unlock new areas?
▼
Progress through the game by successfully scaring inhabitants in each area. As you clear rooms, new areas of the house become accessible, each with new objects to possess and challenges to overcome.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes, as you progress through the game, inhabitants become more resistant to basic scares. You'll need to combine different haunting techniques and use more complex strategies to succeed in later levels.
