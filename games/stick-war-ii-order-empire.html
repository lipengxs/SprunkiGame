Frequently Asked Questions

What's new in Stick War II compared to the original?
▼
Stick War II introduces new units, enhanced graphics, expanded empire management features, and a deeper technology tree. The game also includes multiplayer modes and a more extensive campaign story.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! The game features both cooperative and competitive multiplayer modes where you can battle against friends or team up to take on AI opponents.
How do I unlock new units and technologies?
▼
Progress through the campaign, complete missions, and gather resources to unlock new units and technologies. The technology tree system allows you to choose your empire's development path.
Are there regular updates?
▼
Yes, the game receives regular updates with new content, balance changes, and features to keep the gameplay fresh and exciting!
