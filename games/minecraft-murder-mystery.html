About The Game

Minecraft: Murder Mystery brings the thrill of detective work to the beloved Minecraft universe. In this multiplayer mystery game, players must work together to identify the murderer among them while collecting clues and staying alive. Each round brings new challenges and suspense in this exciting browser-based adventure.
