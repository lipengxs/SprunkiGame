Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start my prehistoric park?
▼
Begin with the comprehensive tutorial that guides you through basic park management, including creating your first dinosaur exhibit and understanding essential security measures. You'll learn the fundamentals while building your park's foundation.
What dinosaur species are available?
▼
The game features a wide range of dinosaur species from different prehistoric eras, including herbivores and carnivores. Through genetic research, you can unlock and create new species, each with unique behaviors and requirements.
How do I keep my park safe?
▼
Safety is crucial in Dinosaur Park. Implement proper security measures, maintain strong fences, monitor dinosaur happiness, and have emergency protocols ready. Regular facility maintenance and staff training are essential for preventing incidents.
What makes this game unique?
▼
Dinosaur Park combines detailed park management with prehistoric authenticity. The game features advanced genetics systems, realistic dinosaur behaviors, and complex ecosystem management, creating an immersive prehistoric park experience.
