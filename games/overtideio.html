Frequently Asked Questions

How do I control my ship?
▼
Use WASD or arrow keys to navigate your ship. Left click to fire your main weapons, and right click for special abilities. Each ship class has unique controls and capabilities!
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can team up with friends by joining the same server or creating a private room. Form alliances and coordinate strategies to dominate the seas together!
How do I unlock new ships?
▼
New ships can be unlocked by earning experience points and currency through battles. The more you play and win, the more ships and upgrades become available!
