Frequently Asked Questions

How do I customize my night?
▼
Before starting each night, you can select which animatronic characters to activate and set their individual AI levels. This allows you to create challenges that match your skill level or focus on specific gameplay mechanics you want to master.
What happens if I run out of power?
▼
Running out of power is extremely dangerous! When this happens, your security systems will shut down, making you vulnerable to the animatronics. Managing your power usage is crucial for survival.
How do I earn achievements?
▼
Achievements can be earned by completing specific challenges, surviving with certain combinations of animatronics, maintaining high efficiency with power usage, and discovering special events or easter eggs in the game.
