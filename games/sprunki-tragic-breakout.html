Frequently Asked Questions

How do I solve difficult puzzles?
▼
Look for clues in your environment, experiment with different approaches, and remember that timing is often key. The game provides subtle hints if you get stuck.
Are there different endings?
▼
Yes! The game features multiple endings based on your choices and how many secrets you discover throughout your escape.
Can I save my progress?
▼
The game automatically saves at checkpoints throughout each level, allowing you to resume your escape attempt from the last safe point.
