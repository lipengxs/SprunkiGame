Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start playing Squid Game Playground?
▼
Simply click the Play Now button to join a game. You'll be matched with other players and guided through each mini-game challenge. Practice mode is available for beginners to learn the mechanics.
What happens if I lose a game?
▼
If you lose a game, you're eliminated from the current round. However, you can immediately join a new game or practice specific challenges in practice mode to improve your skills.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can create or join custom games with friends, or team up in Team Mode to work together through the challenges. Use the invite system to bring friends into your game.
How do I earn rewards?
▼
Rewards are earned by surviving rounds, winning games, and completing achievements. These rewards can be used to unlock new costumes, emotes, and other customization options.
