Sprunked Parasite combines survival horror with musical creativity in a unique and terrifying experience. How does the musical combat system work?
▼

▼

Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! The co-op mode allows you to team up with friends to survive the parasitic invasion together, combining your musical abilities to overcome challenges.
