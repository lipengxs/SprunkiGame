About The Game

Sprunki x Melophobia is a unique psychological horror experience where music becomes both your salvation and your torment. Explore a twisted world where sound shapes reality, and silence harbors unspeakable horrors. Use your wits and musical intuition to solve puzzles, avoid dangers, and uncover the dark truth behind your fear of music. With atmospheric sound design, mind-bending puzzles, and a haunting narrative, every moment tests your courage and sanity.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need headphones to play?
▼
While the game can be played without headphones, we highly recommend using them for the best experience. Headphones enhance the immersive sound design and help you solve audio-based puzzles more effectively.
How scary is the game?
▼
The game focuses on psychological horror rather than jump scares. The fear comes from the atmosphere, sound design, and the way music affects the game world. Players can adjust certain horror elements in the settings.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes, the game offers multiple difficulty settings that affect puzzle complexity, horror intensity, and sound-based challenge difficulty. Choose the level that best suits your experience and comfort.
What makes this game unique?
▼
Sprunki x Melophobia combines psychological horror with innovative sound-based gameplay mechanics. The way music and sound interact with the game world creates a unique and immersive horror experience.
