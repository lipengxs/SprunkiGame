Frequently Asked Questions

How do I learn new spells?
▼
New spells can be learned through various methods: completing tower challenges, finding spell tomes, training with master sages, and reaching specific character levels.
What classes are available?
▼
The game features six main classes: Elementalist, Mystic, Battle Mage, Sage, Enchanter, and Spellblade. Each class has unique abilities and specializations.
How high is the tower?
▼
The main story tower has 100 floors, each with unique challenges and bosses. The endless tower mode allows you to climb indefinitely with increasing difficulty.
Is there multiplayer?
▼
Yes! You can battle other players in the Arena Mode, trade items and spells, and participate in special tower events with other sages.
