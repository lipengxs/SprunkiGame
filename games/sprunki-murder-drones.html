About The Game

Enter the dark and thrilling world of Sprunki: Murder Drones, where you take control of advanced combat drones in a dystopian sci-fi universe. Navigate through challenging environments, engage in intense aerial battles, and uncover a mysterious conspiracy. With cutting-edge drone technology at your disposal, you'll need to master both combat and stealth to survive. The game features an immersive storyline, multiple drone types with unique abilities, and a mix of action and puzzle-solving gameplay.

Frequently Asked Questions

How many different drone types are there?
▼
There are four main drone types: Combat, Stealth, Support, and Guardian. Each type has unique abilities and can be upgraded through the tech tree.
Is there a storyline to follow?
▼
Yes! The game features an immersive storyline where you uncover a mysterious conspiracy while mastering different drone abilities and completing various missions.
How do I upgrade my drones?
▼
Drones can be upgraded through the tech tree system. Complete missions and challenges to earn upgrade points, which can be spent on new abilities and improvements.
What game modes are available?
▼
The game offers multiple modes including Story Mode, Combat Arena, Stealth Missions, and Challenge Mode. Each mode provides unique objectives and gameplay experiences.
