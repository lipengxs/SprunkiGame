Frequently Asked Questions

How do parallel dimensions work?
▼
Each dimension represents a unique musical reality with its own instruments, sounds, and rules. You can create music in individual dimensions or mix elements across multiple realities to create unique cross-dimensional compositions.
Can I mix sounds from different dimensions?
▼
Yes! The Reality Mixer tool allows you to blend sounds and effects from different dimensions. Experiment with various combinations to create unique cross-dimensional music that couldn't exist in a single reality.
What are dimensional effects?
▼
Dimensional effects are special processors that can alter how sounds interact across different realities. Use them to create unique transitions, blends, and transformations between dimensional elements.
How does reality synchronization work?
▼
The Sync Engine allows you to align and coordinate musical elements across multiple dimensions. Create complex arrangements where different realities interact and complement each other in perfect harmony.
