Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock new Brawlers?
▼
You can unlock new Brawlers through Brawl Boxes earned by playing matches, completing quests, and progressing through the Brawl Pass. Each Brawler has different rarities, making some more challenging to obtain than others!
What are the different game modes available?
▼
Brawl Stars features various game modes including Gem Grab (3v3 collection), Showdown (battle royale), Brawl Ball (team sports), Heist (safe cracking), Bounty (team deathmatch), and more. Each mode offers unique objectives and strategies!
How does the progression system work?
▼
Progress by earning Trophies in matches, upgrading Brawlers with Power Points, and unlocking Star Powers and Gadgets. The Brawl Pass offers additional rewards and exclusive content as you complete seasonal challenges!
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can team up with friends in most game modes, join or create a Club to meet other players, and participate in Club League events together. The game supports cross-platform play for maximum accessibility!
