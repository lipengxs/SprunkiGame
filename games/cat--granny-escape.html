About The Game

Cat & Granny: Escape is an exciting adventure puzzle game where you play as a clever cat trying to escape from a mysterious granny's house. Use your feline agility and wit to solve puzzles, avoid traps, and find your way to freedom. With multiple levels of increasing difficulty, stealth mechanics, and engaging storyline, this game offers hours of thrilling gameplay.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I save my progress?
▼
The game automatically saves your progress after completing each level. You can also manually save at designated checkpoints throughout the game.
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes! The game offers multiple difficulty settings, from Easy to Hard mode, each with different levels of challenge and granny AI behavior.
How many levels are there?
▼
The main story mode features 30 unique levels, with additional levels available in other game modes and through regular updates.
Can I customize my cat character?
▼
Yes, you can unlock various cat skins and accessories as you progress through the game and complete achievements.
