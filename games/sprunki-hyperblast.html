Frequently Asked Questions

How does the rhythm combat system work?
▼
The rhythm combat system syncs your attacks with the beat of the music. Time your moves correctly to perform powerful combos and special moves. The better your rhythm, the more devastating your attacks become!
Can I create my own music while playing?
▼
Yes! The freestyle mode allows you to create your own beats while fighting. Your musical choices directly influence the combat mechanics and special effects in the game.
Is there multiplayer support?
▼
Absolutely! Challenge other players in competitive modes, participate in tournaments, or team up in cooperative missions. The multiplayer experience adds a whole new dimension to the rhythm combat!
What kind of progression system is there?
▼
The game features a deep progression system where you can unlock new moves, abilities, customization options, and music tracks. Level up your character and discover powerful combinations to master the rhythm combat!
