Frequently Asked Questions

How do I improve my accuracy?
▼
Practice is key! Pay attention to wind direction and distance, use the scope's markings effectively, and remember to control your breathing while taking shots. Each weapon has unique characteristics that you'll need to master.
What types of missions are available?
▼
The game features various mission types including elimination missions, protection assignments, stealth operations, and challenge scenarios. Each mission type requires different tactics and approaches.
Can I customize my weapons?
▼
Yes! You can customize your sniper rifles with different scopes, barrels, and attachments. Each modification affects weapon performance, allowing you to tailor your loadout to your playstyle.
