About The Game

Octopus Invasion is an exciting underwater action game that puts you in charge of defending your territory against waves of intelligent octopi. Use a variety of weapons and strategies to protect your base while exploring the depths of the ocean. The game combines fast-paced action with strategic elements to create an engaging underwater adventure.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I defend against the octopi?
▼
Use your arsenal of weapons strategically! Place defenses in key locations and upgrade them as you progress. Different weapons are effective against different types of octopi.
Are there different game modes?
▼
Yes! You can play in survival mode to test your endurance, campaign mode to follow the story, or challenge mode for specific missions.
How do I unlock new weapons?
▼
New weapons are unlocked by progressing through levels and collecting resources. You can also earn them by completing special challenges and achievements.
