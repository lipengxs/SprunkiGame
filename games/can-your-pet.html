Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start caring for my pet?
▼
Begin by customizing your pet's appearance, then follow the in-game tutorial to learn about feeding, grooming, and playing with your virtual companion!
What activities can I do with my pet?
▼
You can feed your pet different treats, play mini-games, dress them up in various outfits, and groom them to keep them happy and healthy!
Are there any surprises in the game?
▼
Yes! The game features unexpected twists and turns that will keep you engaged. We won't spoil the surprises - you'll have to play to find out!
Is this game suitable for children?
▼
While the game has a cute and friendly appearance, it may contain some surprising elements. Parental guidance is recommended!
