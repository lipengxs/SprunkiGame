About The Game

Dungeon Master: Cult & Craft is an engaging dungeon management RPG where you take on the role of an aspiring Dungeon Master. Design and expand your underground lair, recruit cultists, craft powerful artifacts, and manage your growing empire of darkness. With deep crafting mechanics and strategic management elements, every decision shapes your dungeon's destiny.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start building my dungeon?
▼
Begin by selecting a location for your dungeon entrance and gradually expand underground. You'll start with basic rooms and unlock more advanced options as you progress. Remember to balance resource management with expansion!
What types of cultists can I recruit?
▼
There are various types of cultists available, each with unique abilities and specializations. You can recruit workers, crafters, warriors, and mystics. Each type contributes differently to your dungeon's growth and success.
How does the crafting system work?
▼
The crafting system allows you to create items, artifacts, and equipment. Gather resources, research recipes, and use your crafting stations to produce various items. Higher-level cultists can craft more powerful items!
