Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to play Episode 1 first?
▼
While playing Episode 1 will give you more background on the story and characters, Episode 2 is designed to be enjoyable even for new players. The game includes a story recap for those jumping in at Episode 2.
How do I switch between characters?
▼
You can switch between available characters at designated checkpoints throughout the cave system. Each character has unique abilities that help solve different puzzles and access new areas.
Are there multiple endings?
▼
Yes! The game features multiple endings based on your choices and the secrets you discover throughout your journey. Explore thoroughly to unlock all possible endings!
