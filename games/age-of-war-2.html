Frequently Asked Questions

How do I advance to the next age?
▼
Collect enough experience points and gold through battles, then click the advance button when available. Each age advancement unlocks new units and technologies!
What's the best strategy for beginners?
▼
Focus on balancing your economy and military. Start with basic units, upgrade your base defenses, and save resources for age advancements.
How do I unlock special abilities?
▼
Special abilities are unlocked through research and age advancement. Each age has unique abilities that can turn the tide of battle!
Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes! Choose from Easy, Normal, and Hard difficulties. Each level affects enemy AI behavior and resource generation rates.
