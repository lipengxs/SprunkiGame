Frequently Asked Questions

How many players can join a game?
▼
Each game can host up to 100 players simultaneously. The minimum number of players required varies by game mode, ranging from 10 to 50 players.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can invite friends to join your game or create a private room. Team mode allows you to form alliances with up to 4 other players.
How do I earn rewards?
▼
Rewards can be earned by winning games, completing daily challenges, participating in events, and climbing the leaderboard rankings.
Are there regular updates?
▼
Yes, the game receives regular updates with new mini-games, cosmetic items, special events, and gameplay improvements based on player feedback.
