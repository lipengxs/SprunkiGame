About The Game

Grand Escape: Prison is an intense prison break simulation game that challenges your strategic thinking and quick decision-making skills. As a prisoner seeking freedom, you must carefully plan your escape route, gather necessary tools, and avoid vigilant guards. The game combines stealth mechanics with puzzle-solving elements to create an engaging escape experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start planning my escape?
▼

Are there different difficulty levels?
▼
Yes! The game offers multiple difficulty levels to suit different player skills. As you progress, you'll face more challenging prison layouts and smarter guard AI.

▼
Absolutely! The game automatically saves your progress at checkpoints, allowing you to resume your escape attempt at any time.
