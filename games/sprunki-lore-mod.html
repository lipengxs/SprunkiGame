Frequently Asked Questions

How much new content does the Lore Mod add?
▼
The Lore Mod adds extensive content including new stories, character backgrounds, historical events, and hidden secrets throughout the game world. Players can expect hours of new narrative content to explore.
Is this mod suitable for new players?
▼
Yes! While longtime fans will appreciate the deeper dive into the universe, new players can enjoy the mod as an enhanced introduction to the Sprunki world.
How do I unlock all the lore content?
▼
Lore content is unlocked through exploration, completing character stories, solving puzzles, and discovering hidden locations throughout the game world.
Are there multiple storylines to explore?
▼
Yes! The mod features multiple narrative paths, character-specific storylines, and branching story options that allow for different experiences with each playthrough.
