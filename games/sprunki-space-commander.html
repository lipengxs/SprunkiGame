Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start building my fleet?
▼
Begin by establishing your first space station and shipyard. From there, you can construct various ship types ranging from small scouts to massive battleships. Each ship class has unique strengths and roles in your fleet.
What resources do I need to manage?
▼
The main resources include minerals, energy crystals, and rare elements. You'll need to establish mining operations, trade routes, and manage your economy to maintain a steady supply of these essential resources.
Can I play with friends?
▼
Yes! You can form alliances with other players, engage in cooperative missions, and participate in massive multiplayer battles. The alliance system allows for shared resources and coordinated fleet operations.
